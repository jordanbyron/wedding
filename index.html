How They Met

Jordan and Kelly meet in the fall of 2004 while attending the University of New Haven. They got to know each other as Kelly needed music cut for the dance team and Jordan and Best Man Bobby were recruiting for the UNH Marching Band program. The romance blossomed around Kelly’s birthday in November and the couple had fallen head of over heals by Christmas aka "falling in love time".

The Proposal

Fast forward about 7 and ½ years later, Jordan and Kelly went for a run along the boardwalk after dinner on July 25, 2012; an otherwise uneventful Wednesday. About a half mile into the run, Jordan needed to take a break, feigning some kind of ill feeling and Kelly of course was unsympathetic asserting that they should have waited longer to run after eating. All of a sudden, before Kelly realized what was happening, Jordan was holding her hands and telling her how much he loved her and then he was down on one knee.... "falling in love time".
